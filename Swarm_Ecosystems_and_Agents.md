# 🐝 Koola-10 Swarm Ecosystems & Specialist Agents

The Koola-10 ecosystem is composed of 7 primary specialized swarms (and additional verticals), each containing a fleet of 10 autonomous agents.

---

## 1. 🪙 Sterling Ecosystem (Finance & Reporting)
**Factory**: `FinancialFactory`
**Roles & Specialties**:
1. **P&L Reporting (Monthly)**: Generates monthly profit and loss statements.
2. **P&L Reporting (Quarterly)**: Aggregates quarterly financial performance.
3. **Cash Flow Statement**: Tracks real-time cash movement.
4. **Cash Flow Forecasting**: Predicts future liquidity based on historical trends.
5. **Investor Update**: Drafts periodic updates for stakeholders.
6. **Investor Presentation**: Creates data-driven slide decks for fundraising.
7. **Board Deck (Operational)**: Focuses on day-to-day metrics for board reviews.
8. **Board Deck (Strategic)**: High-level strategic outlook for board sessions.
9. **Variance Analysis (Budget)**: Compares actual spend against planned budget.
10. **Variance Analysis (Actual)**: Deep-dive into spending discrepancies.

## 2. 🌟 Nova Ecosystem (Lead Generation & Grants)
**Factories**: `LeadGenFactory`, `GrantSwarmFactory`
**LeadGen Roles**:
1. **LinkedIn Scraper (Tech/Finance/Healthcare)**: Targets leads in specific industries.
2. **Crunchbase Enrichment/Deep Dive**: Extracts funding and growth data.
3. **Email Verification**: Ensures lead list deliverability.
4. **ICP Scoring**: Ranks leads by Ideal Customer Profile fit.
5. **Outreach Sequencing**: Manages automated communication flows.
6. **CRM Sync**: Keeps internal databases updated.
**Grant Roles**:
1. **Federal Database Monitor**: Scans Grants.gov for new opportunities.
2. **Federal Proposal Draft/Compliance**: Manages high-stakes federal submissions.
3. **State Grant Search/Draft**: Focuses on regional funding opportunities.
4. **Foundation Outreach/Proposal**: Targets private and family foundations.
5. **Impact Report Gen**: Creates reports to satisfy grant requirements.
